⚡ Situation 4.2: Frozen Screen (Hard-Wired Powered)

← Back to Main Menu
Find and turn OFF boiler isolator switch Does the thermostat restart? If resident cannot find isolator switch Can heating come ON manually? Call Switchee if unresolved

1. Find and turn OFF boiler isolator switch

Guide the resident to find and turn OFF the boiler isolator switch for one minute before switching back ON.

2. Does the thermostat restart?

If yes, check if heating can be controlled. If no, continue troubleshooting.

3. If resident cannot find isolator switch

Book a repairs visit to assist with power cycling the Switchee and boiler.

4. Can heating come ON manually?

Use the red override button on the BRU if needed for heating/hot water while waiting for repairs.

5. Call Switchee if unresolved

Resident or engineer should call Switchee to troubleshoot firmware or arrange thermostat replacement.
