Can resident turn OFF hot water on thermostat? Is the BRU red light above hot water symbol ON? Use override button to turn OFF hot water Check if boiler is still firing after OFF Reset boiler if needed Organize engineer visit if problem persists

1. Can resident turn OFF hot water on thermostat?

Guide the resident to manually turn OFF the hot water using the Switchee thermostat hot water button.

2. Is the BRU red light above hot water symbol ON?

Check if the BRU still shows hot water ON light after thermostat is set to OFF.

3. Use override button to turn OFF hot water

If the BRU light remains ON, press the red override button once to manually switch OFF hot water.

4. Check if boiler is still firing after OFF

Confirm with resident if the boiler still fires after hot water and override are OFF. If so, continue to next steps.

5. Reset boiler if needed

Turn OFF boiler isolator switch for 1 minute to reset boiler if system is stuck.

6. Organize engineer visit if problem persists

If boiler still misbehaving after reset, book housing association repairs and contact Switchee support at 0800 133 7957.
